    Argentina

    <<<<<<< HEAD info | comments ======= info | comments >>>>>>> addingMoreInfo

    Information

    Business

    • Personal relationships should be made before business is done, some small talk is common before business talk.
    • Negotiations are normally do not come easily; there will most likely be multiple meetings to settle a deal.
    • Contracts are lengthy and detailed, everything will be in writing.
    • Meetings should be confirmed a week in advance

    Greetings

    • Handshakes are common when first meeting someone.
    • Titles are extremely important, especially in the presence of an elder. (Ex.Teacher="Professor", One with a PhD="Dr.", Lawyers="Abogado")
    • Greeting with a hug or kiss is considered a compliment to a woman

    Social Conditions

    • Flirtatious commentary and “catcalling” is very common. Men may call out to you while walking down the street.

    Attire

    • It is common to dress up a little more, even for casual events.
    • Women dress in more feminine clothing and will typically not wear sneakers or athletic wear in general.
