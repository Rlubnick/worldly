    U.S.A

    info | comments

    Information

    Business

    • American women have a strong sense of independence.Special help based on gender is perceived as offensive.
    • American women pride themselves on the number of responsibilities they take have.Their career choices span from business to education to government.

    Greetings

    • One would typically greet another with “Hey! Whats up?” or “Hey! How are you?”
    • It has become increasingly popular for American women to either keep their maiden names or use both maiden and married names after marriage. But, when addressing a woman, use the title "Ms." unless you know that she prefers "Mrs." or "Miss."

    Social Conditions

    • Saying “Thanks” or “Thank You” is very common, even for a small act such as holding open the door.

    Attire

    • In many casual settings, it is very common for people to dress in sports clothes, blue jeans are also considered casual and is extremely common.
    • Wearing fur in America is largely criticized because of the suffering and pain inflicted on animals involved.

    Canada

    info | comments

    Information

    Business

    • At a business meeting, introductions should be accompanied by a handshake and strong eye contact.
    • When exchanging business cards, they should have both French and English translations.
    • For business-wear, men typically wear suits and ties while women wear suits or dresses.

    Greetings

    • When meeting someone in canada, it is appropriate to share last names and titles.
    • In Quebec it is not uncommon to kiss once on both cheeks, older men may even kiss a woman’s hand.

    Social Conditions

    • When a woman enters or leaves a room it is expected that men rise because it shows politeness.

    Attire

    • Canadian dress varies depending on regions. French Canadians reflect a more relaxed European style.
    • The climate in canada also reflects outerwear. Many people will dress in layers, which has inspired wearing sweaters or cardigans over shirts.

    Mexico

    info | comments

    Information

    Business

    • Men who give compliments in the workplace are common and may even be flirtatious towards women. As a women accept the compliment with respect while firmly reminding your male counterparts that you are a businesswoman.
    • Women should prepare for some difficulty when doing business in Mexico because some businessmen may not have many dealings with women in positions of authority. To deal with this, demonstrate your competence, skill and authority.
    • It is unusual for foreign businesswomen to invite Mexican businessmen to dinner unless their spouses are available to come.

    Greetings

    • Men typically bow when greeting a Mexican woman.Only shake hands only if she extends her hand first.

    Social Conditions

    • Gestures considered inappropriate and sexist in the United States is also considered sexist and inappropriate in Mexico.

    Attire

    • In general, both men and women are expected to dress conservatively. Colors such as navy and dark gray are highly recommended.
    • Women are also recommended to wear make-up.
